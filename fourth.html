T90Official is an American streamer / content creator / tournament organizer who is widly considered one of the main factors for growing the player-base and audience of the game. He has recurring series such as "Low Elo Legends" where he casts lower level players playing and gives commentary on ways to improve performances. Most succesful event he organized was the Hidden Cup 3 in the beginning of 2020 with 50 000 $ prize pool, at the time the biggest overall prize pool in AoE2 history.

TheViper is a Norwegian pro player who is regarded as the best player in the competitive scene since 2013. From 2012 to early 2020 he has won all major 1v1 tournaments and been at least in the top 2 for most team tournaments as well. He has all time highest earning from all AoE2 players having won over 126 000 $ in prize money.

In the recent events TheViper has been challenged more fiercly than before and his winning streak has ended, but while some may dispute whether he is still the number one player his is undoubtetly among the few top players.

DauT is a Serbian pro player who has been playing at top level ever since 2002. He was regarded as number one for some time between 2002 and 2010 and was always included in the discussion for number one spot during that time. Even after close to 20 years of playing he is still firmly in the top 16 and has recently won major team tournaments.
